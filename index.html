To help you get started with your ETL project, we've provided these helpful templates, pre-built workflows, and guides for a number of differet platforms.

Socrata Connector Services

Socrata Connector Services are Socrata-managed, cloud-hosted services that automatically to keep data from the third-party services you make use of in sync with datasets on your Socrata Platform. All you need to do is work with our customer success team to provide connection information, and the rest will happen automatically. Many of them will even make sure your data is pre-formatted for use in one of Socrata's best-in-breed applications. {% include listing.html type='service' %}
